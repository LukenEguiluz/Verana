Activities

There are a variety of activities to enjoy during your stay at Verana, from snorkeling, secluded picnics on the beach, whale watching while also spotting dolphins, turtles and manta rays, or fishing as the sun rises. Maybe a hike upriver to a remote waterfall is more your speed, or perhaps you’d like to partake in a cooking or tequila class. Our activities director will be happy to help you create an experience to remember.

By Land

Take a hike with us and discover the town of Yelapa, nearby waterfalls, local flora and fauna and breathtaking views.

By @darklittlehouse
By Lauren Paez

By Sea

Head out to sea on a maritime safari, go whale watching, snorkel in beautiful coves, explore the coast and discover a pace of life mostly forgotten.

By Hook

Cast your line for tuna, mahi mahi, snapper and more. Bring your catch back, and our Chef will be happy to prepare it for you.

By Us

Become a tequila and mezcal aficionado, try our Mexican wines and let our chef teach you some of our signature dishes. There’s a class to suit every taste.
